About Legal and Administrative Framework

The Legal and Administrative Framework tool is used to inform the user about the legal and administrative framework related to European Structural and Investment Funds (ESIF). This method provides an overview of important aspects related to the European Regional Development Fund (ERDF)regulations and funding projects in the framework of national and regional Operational Programmes (OP).
This method will provide the user with the latest information on legislative and related policy texts covering the smart specialisation design, implementation, and evaluation process.
This method will also support the user by providing tools to visualise ESIF data in multiple forms.
